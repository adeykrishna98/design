TopicIQ provides news feed to enhance your knowledge feed based on learning from your conversations. It can be added as plugin to slack and provide you with relevant content - news, articles, event information for your key topics of interest. TopicIQ is your personal assistant in the pursuit of knowledge; helping you to learn and find information that truly matters to you.

Business Goals

Their objective was to improve overall aesthetic and user experience and to make the site more engaging. Restructured information architecture to better connect their distinct audience.

Our Strategy

TopicIQ’s knowledge feeds use artificial Intelligence and machine learning to provide the relevant content to their users. With this in mind we set out to create a site that offered relevant design, high performance and total flexibility.

Our Approach

We created a website that put TopicIQ expertise at the forefront, showcasing them in an innovative way. We focused on creating intuitive user-journeys and navigation points. We worked hard with colors and styles to represent TopicIQ expertise.
